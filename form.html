PRELIMINARY DEFINITIONS AND NOMENCLATURE

In this Regulation, unless the context specifies otherwise

  1. "Programme" means Degree Programme, i.e. B.E. / B.Tech. Degree Programme.
  2. "Branch" means specialization or discipline of B.E. / B.Tech. Programme like Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, etc.
  3. "Course" means a theory or practical subject like Engineering Physics, Calculus and Differential Equations, Engineering Graphics, etc. that is normally studied in a semester.
